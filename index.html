The Bulletine Project

We are a student-led nonprofit organization that works to support local restaurants/businesses with digital marketing.

Contact Us

How It Started

Our team shares one desire to support the community during this unprecedented global pandemic. eMarketing and online sales have statistically been proven over the recent years to be the forefront of driving revenue, but many continue to lack the resources and skills. The loss of storefront experience during a global pandemic has lost the majority of sales, and even shut down life-long businesses within months.

However, online sales skyrocketed as consumers stayed home.Our team’s experiences with digital media and marketing allowed us to create Bulletine. We witnessed the possibility of creating a digital space to generate opportunity, and we decided we wanted to help buisnesses adapt, modernize, and digitize. All free of cost. We know that not every business owner has the budget, time, or comprehensive knowledge of what to do with digital marketing.

We are ready to get started on supporting your business. Let’s collaborate!

How we want to help

We are passionate to help local restaurants adapt to a new format of sales. We will optimize the use of digital media skills to create engaging, unforgettable impressions on social media for your brand.

We create visually appealing, sales driving campaigns and digital platform media that can be immediately shared to a pre-existing social media account, or a newly created one with our help. This is a 100% free of charge service that our team wants to create to serve our community.

Let us give you a presence online, a broader reach, and increase your profits.

Our support aims to fully adapt your business to a changing modern world for the short-term or long-term. We hope to truly maximize your businesses’ success. Let’s collaborate!

Collaborate for FREE

Our services are entirely free-of-cost. We are a group of UBC students who strive to create open conversations, supportive planning, and multicultural adaptations to positively influence businesses’ short-term and long-term business goals with our three step process

The Three Step Process

STEP 1:

Collaborate

Connect with us through our email, Linkedin, or Instagram page to get started!

STEP 2:

Create

We will work with your current branding and generate new content with innovative ideas with our skilled talent team. We will use our existing sample formats or creating new styles to create content that suits your needs.

STEP 3:

Connect

We will create a social platform that will appeal to your desired audience, perfect your branding, and kickstart your digital presence online within 4 weeks.

What we can do for you

As a nonprofit team, we work collaboratively with our extensive experience in photography, digital marketing, and graphic design. Through our simple three-step process, we will generate content unique to your brand. We provide personalized designs for the most refined presentation of your products. We will work to design your most ideal concept through our collaboration.
